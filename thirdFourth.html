Supply List

  • Bible-NIV
  • 2 reams, copy paper
  • 24 ct Crayon (no bigger, to share)
  • 1pk Pencils (to share) students may bring mechanical pencils also.
  • 2 pkg pencil top erasers (to share)
  • 2 green or blue colored pens
  • Scissors (Fiskars)
  • Glue bottle or glue stick (Elmers works best)
  • 12" ruler (not collapsible or bendable)
  • Wide ruled, Filler Notebook paper (through-out the year)
  • 6 (3 prong) pocket folders, at least one should be plastic.
  • Please have these available the first day of school.
  • 3 composition notebooks
  • Kleenex (2 boxes to be shared through out the year)
  • 1 pkg multi-colored highlighters
  • 1 set broad tip markers, to share.
  • 1 pkg of reward candy or individually wrapped snacks

Welcome to Cornerstone's third and fouth grade! My name is Katie Pavao (like Ka-pow!) and this will be my 18th year teaching. I completed my undergraduate work at Eastern Washington University with a BA in elementary education. I majored in math and I am endorsed in English K-12. I am currently working on another endorsement in reading.

I have been married for 21 years and have two amazing daughters. I have been a Christian for 28 years and have attended Life Center for 15 years. I enjoy camping with my family, scrapbooking and stamping.

Teaching is a joy and passion of mine. I am very interested in making this a successful and happy school year for you and your child and the plans God has in store for us this year.
