  • 24 count box of crayola crayons
  • colored pencils or markers for projects
  • #2 pencils (mechanical pencils are fine)

  • Scissors (Fiskars)
  • Glue bottle and Glue stick (preferably Elmer’s)

  • Wide-ruled filler notebook paper (throughout year)

  • (2) Composition notebooks (If you were in 5th last year you can add to the notebooks we started)

Hi, my name is Jennifer Mulcahy. My husband, Fred, and I have four children ranging in age from 7 to 20 years old. We live outside Spokane and love the outdoors and wildlife. My relationship with the Lord and my family are most important to me. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my family, stamping, and being crafty.

I graduated from Eastern Washington University in 1993 with a BA in Education. I majored in reading with a minor in math. I have been with CCA since it began in 2000. It has been fun to watch it grow over the years. At CCA I have taught at different levels ranging from 3rd grade to 6th grade. I love the students here and have watched many of them grow into amazing adults. It is so exciting to look forward to what God has in store for CCA and these students.

Cornerstone has a special place in my heart. God has blessed me and my family by allowing me and my children to be a part of such a special school.
