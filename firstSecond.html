This is our initial supply list. During the year your student may be asked to bring supplies for special projects. Thank you for your support. I look forward to meeting you all and anticipate a wonderful year.

Dear Parents of first and second graders;

My name is Lisa Martin and I am extremely excited to be here at Cornerstone Christian Academy! I am already praying for this class of amazing kids and for God’s light to shine through all of them as they work through their school year.

A little bit about me: I graduated with my Masters in Teaching from Whitworth University and have many years of experience working with students of all ages. Your kids are in good hands!! I love to make learning relevant, memorable, and fun. Your kids will be learning a lot this year and I will be bringing in things like my guitar, drum, art activities, and games to make school and exciting and enjoyable place to be for everyone!

I am here for you if you have any questions or concerns. It’s best to reach me by email at guitarlisa@yahoo.com . I can also be reached by phone before and after school by calling 835-1235 or feel free to stop in and see me! I’d love to meet you!

Please check your child’s homework folder daily as I will be sending important information home with them periodically. Thank-you.

I’m looking forward to all God has planned for this class this year!

Lisa Martin

1-2 Grade Teacher, Cornerstone Christian Academy
