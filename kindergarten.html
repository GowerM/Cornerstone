Supply List

  • 2 reams, copy paper
  • 1 box #2 pencils
  • 1 boxes, 48 count crayons
  • 1 pad penmanship paper
  • 1 box washable markers
  • 1 pkg construction paper
  • 3 glue sticks and 1 bottle of glue
  • 2 big erasers and 1 pkg pencil top erasers
  • 1 paint shirt
  • 2 boxes, facial tissue (to share w/ class)
  • 2 boxes of baby wipes & 2 refills
  • 1 plastic pocket folder
    • small toys and stickers for the class store
  • 1 pencil box
  • If your child will be napping, please bring in 1 fitted crib sheet, a blanket and small pillow. Please mark all items with your child's name.

This is our initial supply list. Throughout the year you may be asked to bring in items for special projects. During the first week of school each child will be asked to sign up for a month to bring water bottles. Thank you for your support and we look forward to meeting you all and having a wonderful year.

Hello, my name is Julie Murphy and I am the Kindergarten teacher for Cornerstone. I have been a substitute teacher for the past eleven years for pre-school through high school. Most of my experience has been teaching in small rural schools or private Christian schools. I completed my Bachelor of Education at EWU with a major in math and a minor in special education. I also earned my Masters of Education at EWU.

I have been married for 27 years. We have four children, ages 20-26, and a 2 year old granddaughter. I am active at Sprague Community Church in children and women's ministries. I enjoy reading, cross stitching, and walking.

I am excited for the kindergarteners as they begin to read and write. It is a blessing to be in a school where these students also learn about God’s truths and hide His Word in their hearts. We also enjoy time for crafts, recreation, and music. I look forward to teaching the kindergarten class.
